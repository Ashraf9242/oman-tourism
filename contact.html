Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Oman?

Citizens of many countries can obtain a visa on arrival. Check with your local Omani embassy for specific requirements.

What is the best time to visit Oman?

October to April offers the most pleasant weather. Summers can be very hot, especially in desert areas.

Is Oman safe for tourists?

Oman is considered one of the safest countries in the region with very low crime rates and friendly locals.

What should I wear in Oman?

Modest clothing is recommended. Women should cover shoulders and knees, especially when visiting religious sites.
