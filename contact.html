Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Oman?

Visa requirements vary by nationality. Many countries can obtain a visa on arrival or online e-visa.

What's the best time to visit Oman?

October to April offers the most pleasant weather for tourism activities.

Is Oman safe for tourists?

Yes, Oman is considered one of the safest countries in the Middle East for tourists.
